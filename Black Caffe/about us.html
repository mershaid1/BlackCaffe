..About Black Caffe..

Black Caffe, a store for drinks, hookahs, sweets, food and ice cream, started in 2020 as a small shop selling take away drinks only, hot and cold, to cafe known for everything it offers now.

It was founded by Bahaa Al-Quneiri, a young man in his twenties from the city of Jenin, and he started interacting on social media to attract people to try the delicious drinks he offers, and he gathered around him a distinguished staff to serve the customer in the best way.
